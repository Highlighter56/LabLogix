LABLOGIX

LabLogix is a real-time tracking app built for Computer Science students who want to make the most of their time. Our university labs are packed with powerful resources, like high-performance computers, 3D printers, and more. Students are currently underutilizing free resources as a result of not having information.

LabLogix solves that problem by giving you a live view of open seats, current occupancy, and device availability across all lab rooms. Whether you need a quick workstation, or planning a longer study session and want to know the best time to come in, LabLogix gives you the information you need.

LabLogix puts the lab in the palm of your hand, so you can focus less on finding a spot and more on getting things done.

Developers: Fabjona Stoja, Michelle Vool, Jenn Zhang, Patrick Hughes
